# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be a friendly, productive place to collaborate.

## Be respectful

- Assume good intent
- Be kind and constructive in reviews and discussions
- Critique code, not people

## No harassment

Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated in any project space
(issues, PRs, discussions, chats).

## Privacy

Do not share private information (emails, addresses, logs containing secrets, etc.)
without explicit permission.

## Reporting

If something happens:

- Sensitive reports: **newerasowwor@gmail.com**
- Non-sensitive: open an issue with minimal details, or contact maintainers privately.

Maintainers may remove content and restrict participation to enforce this policy.
